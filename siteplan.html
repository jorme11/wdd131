Star Quilting Site Plan

Site Name

Star Quilting

This name reflects the creative and artistic focus of the business, which specializes in custom quilt designs — especially patterns featuring stars, a popular motif in quilting. The name is simple, memorable, and aligns well with the site's target audience: quilters and customers looking for professional quilting services and inspiration.

Optional domain availability: starquilting.com (availability not required for this class)

Site Purpose

The Star Quilting site provides a portfolio of custom quilting projects, pricing and service information for longarm quilting services, and a gallery of quilting examples. It serves both new and experienced quilters, and anyone looking for quilting services or ideas. Visitors will be able to browse the gallery, read FAQ answers, and send an email to ask questions regarding commission work.

Scenarios

  • How much does it cost to have my quilt top professionally quilted?
  • Can I see some examples of past quilts done by this studio?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #1B4F4D – Used for headings, navigation text, and main brand elements.
  • Accent Color: lightblue – Used for backgrounds of headers, buttons, and callouts.

The color palette is soft yet professional to reflect the calming and creative nature of quilting.

Typography

  • Josefin Sans – Used for all body text and navigation links for clean readability.
  • Shadows Into Light – Used for headings and special titles to give a handwritten, friendly, and artistic tone.
